Basketball

Basketball is one of the most popular sports that is played mostly in America and Canada. The players have to shoot the Basketball into the hoop to score points. There are certain time limits to do so. At the end of the game, the one with the most points win.

The History: From where it all began.

Basketball was invented by an American a Canadian teacher named James Naismith. It was invented in New York in November 1st,1986. He invented this sport because he wanted his students to stay fit during the cold so he came up with the idea of creating the sport that we know today Basketball.

Popular basketball leagues.

One of the most famous leagues in basketball history is the NBA. It is a basketball league in where 30 different teams compete against each other to win a championchip. 15 teams are in the Eastern conference and 15 in the western conference. All teams play 82 games and then the top 8 teams who have the best record in each conference, Move to the playoffs. They play a best of 7 series in which the team to first win 4 games wins the series. This happenes when there is only 1 team left in each conference. These two teams compete for the real championchip.The winner gets a trophy and all the players on The winning teams get their own rings.

Rules.

Here are some of the basic basketball rules.

1. Travel: A travel happens when you don't dribble the ball after you took three steps.
2. Double drible: When you dribble the ball with both of your hands at the same time. Or if you dribble, and you stop dribbling and you dribble again.
3. Fouls: When you push,tackle a person and more.
4.Shot-clock violation: This is when you and your team take more than 24 seconds to shoot.
These are some basic basketball rules.
